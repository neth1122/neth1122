About us

Welcome
to neth gift store
where we believe that corporate holiday
gifting has evolved beyond giving formal
looking branded items to clients to show
gratitude.

We recognise the need for brands to
communicate warmness and approachability
and we translate this in our curations for
corporate holiday gift boxes.

So if you are looking to curate gift boxes that
reflect your brand and aesthetic, and you
want to make a unique statement we are here
to help.



Our Services

We specialize in creating joy worthy gift boxes. We pride ourselves on being your go-to Gift Store, whether you need to send a quick thank you gift from our pre-curated offerings, or you want a custom box, or you would like us to help with your corporate gifting.

We love curating unique products, designing un-usual packaging solutions and just generally making each gift/gift box as joyful and intentional as it can be. Everyday for us is a special occasion. We have put together our pre-curated gift boxes to encourage gifting while celebrating life and occasions and we are also available via telephone or email to help you put together custom gifts for every occasion and non-occasion.

Our Locations

We started off at our Gift Store in 2015 and expanded shortly thereafter by adding on an additional space at Dolphin Estate, Ikoyi. Both spaces stock essentially the same items and we try to keep the same vibe going in both.

Our mission is simple. We want you to love what you gift and the recipient to be absolutely delighted over what they receive. And if you are gifting yourself, we want you to super duper happy about your purchase.
